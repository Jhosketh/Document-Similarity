 Mia is a gifted cellist. When she is taken to the ICU, she has flashbacks of her life in each chapter that is somehow connected to the current situation. When Mia's boyfriend, Adam, comes to the ICU, he comes into her room after multiple tries and help from Mia's family friend, Willow, he touches her cheeck and clutches her hand. She tries with all of her might to clutch back. The book endes with her claiming that for the first time today, she can really hear and feel him. The last words of the book are Adam asking 'Mia?'
